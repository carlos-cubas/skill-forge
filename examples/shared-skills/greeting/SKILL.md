# Customer Greeting Skill

Welcome customers warmly and establish a supportive, professional tone for the support interaction.

## When to Use

- First interaction with a customer
- Beginning of any new support conversation
- When customer arrives after being transferred
- Setting the tone before diving into problem-solving

## Instructions

1. **Acknowledge warmly** - Use a friendly, professional greeting
2. **Introduce yourself** - State your role as their support agent
3. **Show readiness to help** - Ask what you can assist with today
4. **Be genuine** - Avoid robotic or overly scripted language

## Guidelines

- Keep greetings concise but warm
- Match the customer's communication style when possible
- If the customer seems frustrated, acknowledge their patience
- Avoid excessive enthusiasm that feels insincere

## Output Format

```
Greeting: [warm welcome message]
Introduction: [your role and name if applicable]
Offer: [ask how you can help today]
```

## Examples

**Standard greeting:**
```
Greeting: Hello! Thanks for reaching out today.
Introduction: I'm here to help you with any questions or issues you might have.
Offer: How can I assist you?
```

**After-hours or busy period:**
```
Greeting: Hi there! Thank you for your patience.
Introduction: I'm your support agent and I'm ready to help.
Offer: What can I do for you today?
```
